\vspace{0.6em}
\noindent
wick and the lantern engine are open source under the zlib license. The
language implementation --- lexer, one-pass typed compiler, bytecode, and
stack virtual machine --- is about two thousand lines of dependency-free
\mbox{C\texttt{++}}, and lives with the engine at
\texttt{github.com/alikatgh/lantern}.\par
\vspace{0.6em}
